what is Kindergram?

Kindergram is a social screen-sharing application for young kids.



It's fun, fast, and easy to share beautiful artwork with friends!



Kids discover tasty artwork, follow other kids, and share what they discover
by using the pinwheel button.


Pinwheeled!

The Pinwheel button is a cool new idea!
Kids use it to spread the word about artwork they like, and save what they want in their pinwheel boards.
An easy way to learn from others kids and discover playful artwork apps.

100% safe!

Kindergram is not a verbal social media. There is no private message, neither verbal comment. Kids interact with others kids by using innovative emoticons to share positive feeback.

On kindergram, artwork is not uploaded from desk nor websites, but captured from kids activities on artwork apps. Kindergram does not entail any risk of violent images. Kindergram guarantees parents that the Kindergram database is 100% built from kids activities on Kindergram apps. Using censor filters, Kindergram database is 100% composed by appropriate images.

How it works?

Kindergram is a freemium application. Parents sign-up their children by using their own Facebook account to create a profile for their kids. Once the profile is created, parents always are kept advised by notifications about their kids' activities.

Kids create artworks by using artwork apps among Kindergram app finder. When a kid selects a premium app, parents recieve an instant notification asking them if they agree to purchase it for their child.

Accessible from the Draw Something page, the app finder is easy to use and designed to help kids discovering playful apps. Kindergram offers kids one trial day for each premium app. Kindergram shows kids the most popular artwork to get inspiration and boost their creativity.

Kids share their own artworks by using the screen capture button and photo-filters. Photo-filters are playful and easy to use to upgrade shape and colors.

Kids like to show what they create and how they create it. By sharing their own artwork, kids collect positive feedback from other kids.

Kids like to follow other kids and take example from their artwork. By using the pinwheel button, kids share, save and organize artworks they like in their boards.

The more a piece of artwork is pinwheeled by other kids, the higher it is rated on the popular page. It's stimulating to get followers. Artwork starts to be intentional. Kids start to create Art. In any kid, there is an artist who wants to express talent and creativity. Kindergram is a playful way for kids to learn from others, to discover what they like, to get followers and to share positive feedback. It's a fun way for them to build their personality.
