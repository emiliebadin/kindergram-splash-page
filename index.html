    • what is kindergram?

    pinwheel


    It's fun, fast, and easy to share beautiful artwork with friends!
    Kids use the pinwheel button to spread the word about tasty artwork.

    emoticons


    It's playful to share positive feedback!



    No private messages, no verbal comments, kids use innovative emoticons to interact with each other.

    100% safe


    Kindergram does not entail any risk of violent image.

    Pictures are not uploaded from desks nor websites, but captured from kids creativity on doodle applications.

    kindergram app finder

    Kindergram is the first social media for kids that centralizes all kid-friendly applications into one unique App Store. Busy parents don't have to waste time and energy to check and select applications for their child. Kids safely discover new applications by themselves, create artwork on doodle applications among Kindergram App Finder.



    When kids want to use a premium app from Kindergram App Finder, parents get an instant notification to purchase it online. Creativity never waits for a purchase confirmation: Kindergram offers kids one trial day on each premium application.

    how it works?

    sign-up


    Parents sign their children up by using their own facebook account to create a profile for their kids. Once the profile is created, parents always are kept advised by notification about their kids activities. If parents do not have a facebook account, they easily can activate the automatic SMS notification.

    create


    Kids create artwork by using doodle apps among Kindergram App Finder.
    Kindergram offers kids one trial day for each premium application. It's easy to express creativity, and fun to learn from users' creation.

    save


    Kids use the screen capture button to save doodles they create on external apps. It's fun to save and organize artwork in pinwheel boards. Kids build their first childhood database.

    discover


    Kids discover beautiful artwork among the popular page. They follow other kids and take example from them to discover new apps. It's fun to learn from others and to follow their creativity.

    share


    Kids share artwork they create on doodle apps among Kindergram App Finder. They share and organize discoveries in their pinwheel boards. Any web user can use the pinwheel button without signing-up, to spread the word about beautiful artwork among other social media.
